Tax Notices, Litigation
& Representation

Advisory, documentation and representation support across Income Tax and GST proceedings — from notice receipt through resolution.

Please note: Most tax notices carry mandatory response deadlines of 15–30 days. We recommend consulting a CA promptly on receipt of any notice to avoid default or escalation.

Support Through
Tax Proceedings

Tax notices and departmental proceedings require timely, structured responses supported by accurate documentation and a clear understanding of the relevant provisions. How We Work

Our Notice Response Process

01
Notice Review
We review the notice in full, identify applicable legal provisions, assess the scope of the proceedings and advise on the required response.
›
02
Document Checklist
We prepare a structured checklist of records required and assist in organising financial documents, statements and supporting evidence.
›
03
Reply Preparation
A factual and legal reply is prepared with relevant case law, circulars and computation statements, reviewed before submission.
›
04
Submission & Hearings
We handle filing with the department, attend officer hearings on your behalf and manage follow-up correspondence through the proceedings.
›
05
Order & Next Steps
On receipt of the order, we advise on acceptance, rectification or appeal options — and assist with whichever course of action is appropriate.
