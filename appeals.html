Dispute Resolution

Appeals & Litigation

Providing structured representation and advisory services for departmental proceedings under the Income Tax Act and GST framework.

Statutory Timeline Notice Standard tax notices (Section 143, 148, or GST SCNs) typically require responses within 15 to 30 days. We prioritize the review of time-sensitive documents to ensure statutory compliance.

Direct Tax Matters

  • Representation for Scrutiny Assessments (u/s 143(3))
  • Handling Reassessment Proceedings (u/s 148)
  • Filing and Representation for Appeals before CIT (Appeals)
  • Support for ITAT (Income Tax Appellate Tribunal) filings
  • Responses to Penalty and Interest Notices

Indirect Tax (GST)

  • Representation for GST Audits and Investigations
  • Drafting replies to Show Cause Notices (SCN)
  • Handling ITC Mismatch disputes (GSTR-2A/2B vs 3B)
  • Appeals before the GST Appellate Authority
  • Assistance with Search and Seizure proceedings

Representation Methodology

01
Notice Analysis
Technical review of the notice against applicable legal provisions and circulars.
02
Fact Finding
Collation of financial records, ledgers, and third-party reconciliations.
03
Submission
Drafting and electronic filing of factual and legal submissions.
04
Hearings
Representation before the Assessing Officer or Appellate Authority.
05
Closure
Review of final orders and advisory on further appellate remedies.
