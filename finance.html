Finance Is the
Engine of Growth

Whether you're seeking your first working capital line, scaling with a term loan, or restructuring existing debt, getting the financial story right is everything. Banks don't just lend money — they lend it to businesses that can clearly demonstrate repayment capacity, strong cash flows and sound governance. We bridge that gap between your business reality and what bankers need to see.

Credit-Ready Documentation Professionally structured CMA data, projections and financials that speak the banker's language.
End-to-End Loan Facilitation From initial assessment to bank submission, follow-up and sanction support.
Government Scheme Navigation MSME schemes, CGTMSE cover, MUDRA and subsidy advisory for eligible businesses.
Funding Coverage

Types of Finance We Help You Access

Every business has different funding needs. We assess your profile, match you to the right instrument and prepare the documentation to maximise your approval chances.

Working Capital (CC / OD) Cash credit and overdraft limits for day-to-day operational cash flow needs
Term Loans Long-term funding for capital expenditure, expansion and asset acquisition
MSME / SME Loans Priority sector lending with CGTMSE collateral-free cover for eligible businesses
MUDRA Loans Shishu, Kishore and Tarun categories under PM MUDRA Yojana for micro enterprises
Machinery & Equipment Finance Asset-backed loans for plant, machinery, vehicles and technology upgrades
Project Finance Long-gestation capital structures for new business ventures and greenfield projects
Loan Against Property (LAP) Monetise business or personal property to unlock larger loan amounts at lower rates
Debt Restructuring Reorganisation of existing credit facilities to reduce EMI burden and improve cash flow
Core Capabilities

What We Do for Your Business

Bank Loan Assistance
End-to-End Loan Facilitation
  • Assessment of funding eligibility based on financials, credit history and business vintage
  • Identification of appropriate lenders — PSU banks, private banks or NBFCs — for your profile
  • Preparation and compilation of complete loan application dossier
  • Working capital enhancement — CC / OD limit reviews and enhancements at renewal
  • Term loan structuring for capital expenditure with optimal repayment schedules
  • Liaison with bank relationship managers and credit officers through the sanction process
  • Post-sanction compliance — stock statements, quarterly returns, insurance and charge registration
CMA Data & Project Reports
Financial Documentation for Banks
  • Preparation of Credit Monitoring Arrangement (CMA) data as per RBI / bank formats
  • Detailed project financial reports covering promoter background, market analysis and financials
  • 5-year financial projections — P&L, balance sheet and cash flow — with assumptions memo
  • DSCR (Debt Service Coverage Ratio) and break-even analysis to establish repayment capacity
  • Ratio analysis — current ratio, DSCR, TOL/TNW, RONW — presented in bank-ready formats
  • Techno-economic feasibility reports for new projects and greenfield ventures
  • Revision and resubmission support if initial appraisal raises queries
Subsidy & Government Scheme Advisory
MSME Incentives & Grants
  • CGTMSE (Credit Guarantee Fund Trust for MSEs) — collateral-free cover up to ₹2 crore for eligible borrowers
  • MUDRA Yojana facilitation — Shishu (up to ₹50K), Kishore (up to ₹5L) and Tarun (up to ₹10L)
  • Udyam Registration and its impact on priority sector lending eligibility
  • State-specific MSME subsidy schemes — Karnataka Udyog Mitra, KSFC schemes and DIC subsidies
  • Technology Upgradation Fund Scheme (TUFS) for textile and manufacturing units
  • PLI (Production Linked Incentive) scheme eligibility assessment and application support
  • Interest subvention scheme advisory under various Central and State government programmes
Working Capital Optimisation
Cash Flow & Financial Efficiency
  • Cash conversion cycle analysis — identifying bottlenecks in receivables, payables and inventory
  • Debtor and creditor management advisory to improve net working capital position
  • Stock statement preparation and submission as per bank drawing power requirements
  • Invoice discounting and bill discounting facility structuring with banks
  • Factoring and supply chain finance options for faster receivable monetisation
  • Monthly MIS reporting on working capital utilisation and limit adherence
Financial Documentation & Structuring
Compliance & Loan File Management
  • Compilation and attestation of financial statements for bank submission — CA certified as required
  • Net worth certificates for promoters and guarantors
  • Audited accounts preparation and alignment with bank formats
  • ITR and GST return compilation with banking reconciliation support
  • CIBIL / credit score review and advisory on improving borrower credit profile
  • Documentation checklist management to ensure zero-deficiency loan files
NPA Advisory & Loan Restructuring
Stressed Asset Management
  • Early identification of signs of financial stress and pre-NPA advisory to prevent classification
  • One Time Settlement (OTS) negotiations and documentation support with lenders
  • ECLGS (Emergency Credit Line Guarantee Scheme) eligibility review and application support
  • Loan restructuring proposals under RBI guidelines — repayment rescheduling and moratorium structuring
  • Representation before bank committees and review meetings on behalf of borrowers
Our Process

How We Get You Loan-Ready

A structured, step-by-step approach to take your funding requirement from idea to sanction.

01

Business Assessment

We review your financials, credit profile, existing debt and funding requirement to map the right approach.

02

Lender Matching

We identify the most suitable banks or NBFCs based on your sector, loan type, amount and repayment capacity.

03

Documentation

Post-Sanction Compliance

Ongoing support for stock statements, annual renewals, limit enhancements and regulatory reporting.
