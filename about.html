About Us

A Chartered Accountancy firm built on the principles of integrity, independence, and professional insight.

At a Glance

Membership Institute of Chartered Accountants of India (ICAI)
GLOBAL FOOTPRINT India · UAE · UK
CORE EXPERTISE Tax, Audit, Litigation, Virtual CFO, Risk & Internal Control

Sanjay B C & Co is a Chartered Accountancy firm providing professional services in Taxation, Audit, Financial Advisory, Litigation & Virtual CFO, with a focus on compliance, integrity, and client confidentiality.

Leadership

The firm is led by CA Sanjay B C, a member of the Institute of Chartered Accountants of India (ICAI), and adheres strictly to the professional and ethical standards prescribed under applicable regulations.

  • Following professional ethics and regulatory standards
